GeneraList is an infrequent link list email covering a wide range of interests.

While we tend to focus on business and technology, no topic is off limits. Our goal is to make ourselves and our readers smarter and better informed.

We hate most email lists, so we do our best to make this one as engaging and worthwhile as possible. We respect your inbox. We have no intention of spamming you. We only send a list when we feel we have enough interesting content to warrant one. This usually occurs once per month.

Questions, concerns, and interesting links can be directed to generalist@seferdesign.com. GeneraList is edited by Robert Sefer, proprietor of Sefer Design Company.
